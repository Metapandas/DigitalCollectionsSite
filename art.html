ART SELECTIONS

Gigi Aramescu
Gigi Aramescu's work was influenced by Romanian culture and its folkoric arts. Her work is widely acclaimed and it is part of the permanent collection of museums in the United States and Europe.

Elizabeth Catlett
A selection of linoleoum-cut prints and related documentation from the 1984 library exhibition: The Graphics of Elizabeth Catlett.

Emilio Sanchez
The Emilio Sánchez (1921–1999) collection is a reflection of the Cuban-American artist's trajectory, spanning from the 1940s-1980s.

Purvis Young
Purvis Young's artwork reflects on the political, historical, social and mundane aspects of the Miami neighborhood of Overtown.

Gustavo Oviedo
A selection of works of Miami-based artist, diver, environmentalist, and researcher, Gustavo Oviedo.

Gary Monroe
Black-and-white photographs depicting a day in the everyday life of members of two different communities: Little Haiti (1985) and South Beach (1977-1986).

Villasmil & Martinez
A digital repository of the artwork of Edwin Villasmil and Elba Martínez.

David Spitzer
Miami-based Photographer David D. Spitzer captured a variety of subjects, included in this collection: Jazz Artists, Blues Musicians, Pedestrians, Graffiti, Personal Visions, and Reflections.

Garcia & Salazar
Fernando García was part of the nine Cuban-American artists known as The Miami Generation. Rafael Salazar collaborated with García and documented his work through photography.

Vasari Pages
South Florida based artists were invited by curator César Trasobares to create artworks from a page of an 1832 Italian edition of Giorgio Vasari's, "The Lives of the Most Excellent Painters, Sculptors, and Architects."

Bookmarks & Greeting Cards
Various renowned artists collaborated in the 1980s to create artistic bookmarks and greeting cards for the Miami-Dade Public Library System.

Selected Works
Highlights from the Miami-Dade Public Library's Permanent Art Collection, including Picasso and Dali lithographs, among many other interesting artists.

Eduardo Paolozzi
Eduardo Paolozzi is widely considered to be one of the pioneers of Pop Art.

Sculpture in the Round
A collection of sculptures by local artists, divided into series, in which the figures are presented in their complete three-dimensional form.
